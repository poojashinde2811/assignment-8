How Children’s Day is Celebrated

Children’s Day is celebrated with great enthusiasm across India. Schools and institutions organise various events to mark the occasion. These include competitions, cultural programmes, debates, and games, where children get the chance to showcase their talents in activities like music, dance, painting, and essay writing. The celebrations often include giving children gifts such as books, chocolates, and stationery, making the day extra special for them.
